Publications and Presentations

Academic Papers

VIATRA is a long-running project that incorporates the results of over 15 years of active research and development, with a large number of related publications. The following list includes the most important publications related to the current VIATRA

  • Varró, D., Bergmann, G., Hegedüs, Á. et al. Road to a reactive and incremental model transformation platform: three generations of the VIATRA framework.Software and System Modeling 15, 609–629 (2016).
  • Ujhelyi, Z., Bergmann, G., Hegedüs, Á. et al. EMF-IncQuery: An integrated development environment for live model queries. Science of Computer Programming, Volume 98 Part 1, 80-99 (2013)
  • Varró D. Incremental Queries and Transformations: From Concepts to Industrial Applications. In: Freivalds R., Engels G., Catania B. (eds) SOFSEM 2016: Theory and Practice of Computer Science. SOFSEM 2016. Lecture Notes in Computer Science, vol 9587. Springer, Berlin, Heidelberg. (2016)

A large list of related publication are available on the website of the Fault Tolerant Systems Research Group.
